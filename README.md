This is meant to be a fun AI that mimics the player fatslobs play style.

Fatslob plays forest nothing games and has created a unique meta of walling and then creating an army of beserks, scorpions, onogers, and trebuchet's. If you look up t90Offical casts a few of his games on youtube.

To use move fatslob.per, fatslob.ai, and the Fatslob folder into the aoe2 de /resources/_common/ai/fatslob folder.
